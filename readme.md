## 📌 Problem Statement

In many rural and semi-urban areas, tracking individual or family health metrics over time is a major challenge. Minor symptoms or chronic conditions are often forgotten or improperly documented, and previous medical prescriptions are frequently lost. There is no simple and accessible way for citizens or local health workers (such as ASHA workers) to maintain a continuous and localized health audit.

**Arogya-Sahaya** is a **Digital Health Companion** designed to simplify health and medical record management. Users or health workers can register individuals, monitor their health conditions, and securely document symptoms or prescriptions. The application helps maintain accurate health histories that can be easily accessed during medical consultations.

# ✨ Features

## 👤 Patient / Citizen Registration
- Add individual profiles with:
  - Name
  - Age
  - Blood Group
  - Contact Details
  - Profile Photos

---

## 📋 Symptom & Vitals Log
- Record health issues such as:
  - Fever
  - High Blood Pressure
  - Diabetes Symptoms
  - Fatigue
- Add:
  - Date
  - Notes
  - Prescription Details

---

## 📄 Summary Report
- Generate chronological health reports
- Share reports with doctors and healthcare workers
